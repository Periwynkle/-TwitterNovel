THE RULES

1. Click on the latest tweet in the Tweet Tree that you wish to continue from (there many be multiple options if the story has branched).

2. Sign into Twitter, if you aren't signed in already.

3. Reply to the user who authored the latest tweet that you clicked on. Make sure you do not delete the first @username that automatically populates the tweet. Delete any subsequent @usernames that appear.

4. Add #htn #[insertnoveltitlehere] to the beginning of your tweet. (Follow this exact formatting.)

5. You must wait at least 5 minutes before you can post again, and you cannot reply to one of your own tweets (but you can reply to someone who has replied to one of your tweets).

6. Be bold. Take the plunge. And enjoy the ride!
